ADCS supports the annual Neo Art Connect 2024 event inside the Laminor Hall.
Throughout the event, ADCS volunteers will lead first-aid workshops, teaching you to:
- Stay safe
- Check victim consciousness
- Call 112 and describe the situation
- Perform resuscitation maneuvers
- Place a victim in the recovery position

Thanks to Neo Art Connect for the invitation!
