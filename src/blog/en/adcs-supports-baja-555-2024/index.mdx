title: "ADCS Supports Baja 555"
date: "2024-07-23"
description: "Perioada 4–11 august: ADCS asigură primul ajutor pe traseul Ro Rally Marathon."
---

From August 4–11, ADCS volunteer teams will provide first aid throughout the 2,000 km Ro Rally Marathon route:
- 500 km of road
- 1,500 km of rally-raid terrain
We will deploy at least 5 rapid response intervention teams along key points. All volunteers are BLS-certified.

Our team includes:
- ALP Medical Assistants
- Ambulance staff
- Paramedic/Firefighter
- Search and Rescue specialists

A rapid motorcycle intervention team is also available.
